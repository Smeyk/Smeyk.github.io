About me

My name is Guberniuk Oleksii, and I'm live in Ukraine, Kyiv. I'm experienced Web Developer. I began dive in the Front End in early 2016 year, and I keep doing it every day! I create responsive websites that allow the user to experience your website in the best and most appropriate way suited to the device they are using. I write semantic markup that is documented and easy to read means it can be maintained and scaled in the future, and allows co-workers to quickly work with it. My front-end build process involves the use of tools such as Sass, Gulp, Bower, and NPM to speed up development The work I provide is of highest quality, fully responsive, and tested in a wide range of devices.
